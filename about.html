About Sthitha Sthapatya

24 years of creating enduring architecture across Bangladesh and serving NRB families worldwide.

Our Story

Founded on Heritage, Built for the Future

Established in 2002, Sthitha Sthapatya was born from a vision to create architecture that respects Bangladesh's rich cultural heritage while embracing the best of contemporary global design. Our founder, Ar. Rahul Chakraborty, trained at BUET and the Architectural Association in London, bringing a unique cross-cultural perspective to every project.

The name "স্থিত স্থাপত্য" (Sthitha Sthapatya) means "Enduring Architecture" — a philosophy reflected in every structure we design. Over 24 years, we have completed 350+ projects, from private villas in Gulshan to commercial complexes in Chittagong, and served 200+ NRB families seeking to build their dream homes from abroad.
