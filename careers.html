Careers at Sthitha Sthapatya

Join a team that builds enduring architecture and designs spaces that matter — in Dhaka and for clients worldwide.

Why Join Us

Grow With a Award-Winning Practice

We combine rigorous design, hands-on project delivery, and a culture of learning. If you care about craft, client trust, and sustainable practice, you'll fit right in.

📐

Meaningful Projects

Work on residential, commercial, and NRB projects from concept to completion. Our portfolio spans villas, offices, interiors, and heritage-sensitive design.

Local & Global Exposure

Collaborate with a team that works across Bangladesh and with NRB clients in the UK, USA, Middle East, and Australia. Remote-friendly options for the right roles.

Learning & Growth

We support continued learning, RAJUK and regulatory updates, and exposure to 3D visualization, BIM, and project management best practices.
