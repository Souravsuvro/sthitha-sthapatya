NRB Property & Design Services

Your trusted partner in Bangladesh — build, design, and manage your property from anywhere in the world.

For NRBs

We Bridge the Distance Between You and Your Dream Home

Over 200 Non-Resident Bangladeshi families across the UK, USA, Canada, Middle East, and Australia have trusted Sthitha Sthapatya to manage their property projects in Bangladesh. Our dedicated NRB division handles everything — from land verification and architectural design through construction and final handover — so you never need to worry about distance.

We understand the unique challenges NRBs face: time zone differences, trust concerns, regulatory complexity, and the emotional weight of building a family home from thousands of miles away. That's why we built a service model specifically designed for transparency, accountability, and peace of mind.

How We Serve NRBs

Six Pillars of NRB Excellence

Every feature designed to give you complete control and transparency over your project, regardless of where you live.

📹

Remote Consultations

Scheduled video calls via Zoom, WhatsApp, or Google Meet — aligned to your time zone. Initial consultations, design reviews, material selections, and milestone approvals all conducted remotely with screen-sharing and 3D walkthroughs.

📊

Weekly Progress Reports

Every Friday, receive a detailed report with dated photographs, budget status, milestone tracking, upcoming tasks, and risk flags. Reports are delivered via email and WhatsApp — no chasing, no surprises, always up to date.

💱

Dual-Currency Billing

Pay in BDT, USD, GBP, EUR, or AED. All invoices include transparent line-item breakdowns with exchange rate disclosures. Milestone-based payments ensure you only pay for completed work stages.

👤

Dedicated Relationship Manager

Your single point of contact who knows your project inside-out. Available on WhatsApp for quick queries and scheduled calls for detailed discussions. Speaks English and Bangla fluently.

🏠

Virtual Property Tours

360° virtual tours of your property at key milestones — foundation, structure, interior fitout, and pre-handover. Share with family members anywhere in the world for collaborative decision-making.

✈️

Trip Coordination

When you visit Bangladesh, we organise site visits, material showroom tours, and face-to-face design sessions. Airport pickup and full-day project tours included for Premium clients at no extra charge.
