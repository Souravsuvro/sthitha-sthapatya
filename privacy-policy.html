Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

Last updated: March 2026.

Sthitha Sthapatya ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect when you use sthithasthapatya.com, contact us, or use our services, and how we use and safeguard it.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect (a) information you provide: name, email, phone number, address, project details, and any messages you send via our contact form, consultation form, or WhatsApp; (b) usage data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and similar analytics to improve our site; (c) cookies and similar technologies where we use them for essential site function or analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to enquiries, schedule consultations, deliver our architectural and design services, send project updates and invoices, improve our website and services, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We may share data with service providers (e.g. hosting, email) under strict confidentiality. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights. For NRB clients, project-related information may be shared within our team and with contractors as necessary to deliver your project.

4. Data Retention

We retain contact and project-related data for as long as needed to serve you and for legitimate business and legal purposes. You may request deletion of your data subject to applicable law.

5. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Communication over the internet is not fully secure; we encourage sensitive discussions via secure channels when appropriate.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data, or to object to or restrict processing. To exercise these rights or ask questions about this policy, contact us at hello@sthithasthapatya.com.

7. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to third-party sites (e.g. social media, Google Maps). We are not responsible for their privacy practices; please read their policies.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will change; continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact us for any privacy-related questions.
