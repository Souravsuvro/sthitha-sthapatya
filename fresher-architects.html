FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our fresher architect programs.

Who can apply for these programs?

+

Our programs are designed for architecture students in their final year, recent graduates, and architects with up to 2 years of experience looking to enhance their skills and career prospects.

Is there any job placement assistance?

+

Yes! We have a 95% placement rate. We connect our graduates with our network of architecture firms, provide interview preparation, and share job opportunities regularly.

What software will I learn in the 3D course?

+

You'll master AutoCAD, SketchUp, 3D Max, V-Ray, Corona Renderer, and Photoshop post-production techniques used by professional architects.

Can I join multiple programs simultaneously?

+

Absolutely! Many participants combine office sharing with training programs. We offer package discounts for multiple program enrollments.
