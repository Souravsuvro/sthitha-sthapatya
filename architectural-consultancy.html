Architectural Consultancy

End-to-end architectural services from site analysis to construction documentation & RAJUK approvals.

Our Process

5-Phase Design Journey

A proven methodology that transforms your vision into detailed, buildable plans.

Phase 1: Discovery & Site Analysis

Comprehensive site survey, soil assessment coordination, RAJUK regulations review, client brief development, and feasibility study including budget estimation.

Phase 2: Concept Design

Multiple design concepts exploring spatial arrangements, massing, orientation for natural ventilation, and initial 3D visualizations for client review and feedback.

Phase 3: Design Development

Refined architectural drawings, structural engineering coordination, MEP planning, material specification, interior layout integration, and detailed 3D renders.

Phase 4: Approvals & Documentation

RAJUK submission drawings, building permit applications, municipal compliance documentation, and all regulatory coordination until approval is secured.

Phase 5: Construction Support

Tender documentation, contractor selection assistance, periodic site visits, construction quality audits, and design clarifications through project completion.
