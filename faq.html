Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our services, pricing, payment process, and how we work with clients in Bangladesh and abroad.

What services does Sthitha Sthapatya offer?

We offer three core services: Architectural Consultancy (site analysis, design, RAJUK approvals, construction documentation), Luxury Interior Design (space planning, material selection, bespoke furniture, lighting), and Construction Management (contractor coordination, budget and timeline oversight). We also provide 3D visualization, custom furniture design, and dedicated NRB (Non-Resident Bangladeshi) services.

How is architectural consultancy priced?

Pricing is typically per square foot of built-up area. Our Essential package starts from ৳8/sq ft (residential up to 3,000 sq ft), Professional from ৳14/sq ft (residential & commercial), and Premium from ৳22/sq ft (luxury & NRB projects with dedicated architect and weekly updates). Exact quotes depend on scope, location, and deliverables.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept BDT, USD, GBP, EUR, and AED. For NRB clients we offer dual-currency invoicing with transparent exchange rates. Payments can be made via bank transfer, and we use milestone-based billing so you pay at defined stages (e.g. after concept approval, after RAJUK submission) rather than upfront.

How does the payment process work for NRB clients?

NRB projects are billed in milestones (e.g. 20% on concept approval, 30% on design development, 30% on construction documentation, 20% on completion). Invoices are sent by email with a line-item breakdown and current exchange rate. You can pay from your overseas account in USD/GBP/EUR/AED or in BDT. We do not ask for full payment upfront.

Do you work with clients outside Bangladesh?

Yes. Our NRB Services are designed for Non-Resident Bangladeshis. We provide remote consultations (Zoom, WhatsApp, Google Meet), weekly progress reports with photos, a dedicated relationship manager, dual-currency billing, and virtual property tours at key milestones so you can manage your Bangladesh project from anywhere.

How long does RAJUK approval take?

Typical timeline for residential projects in Dhaka is 3–6 months from submission, depending on project size and RAJUK workload. We handle the full submission process, respond to queries, and keep you updated. For commercial or larger projects, timelines can be longer; we provide an estimate during the discovery phase.

What is included in the free 30-minute consultation?

The free consultation is a no-obligation call with our principal architect to discuss your project, site, budget range, and timeline. We give initial guidance on feasibility, approximate costs, and next steps. It helps you decide whether to proceed with a full proposal. You can book via our contact page or WhatsApp.

How much does interior design cost?

Interior design fees typically start from ৳12/sq ft and vary with scope (e.g. full fit-out vs. styling only), number of rooms, and level of customization. We provide a detailed quote after understanding your requirements. Furniture and materials are quoted separately.

What does construction management cost?

Construction management is usually 5–8% of total project value. This covers contractor coordination, material sourcing support, site supervision, progress reporting, budget tracking, and quality checks. It does not include the cost of construction itself.

Do you provide 3D visualizations?

Yes. We offer exterior renders (from ৳25K per view), interior renders (from ৳35K per room), animated walkthroughs (from ৳1.5L per project), and VR tours (from ৳2.5L). These can be bundled with architectural or interior packages or ordered standalone.

Can you help with land verification for NRB clients?

Yes. We assist with land verification, mutation checks, and understanding local regulations before you invest. This is part of our NRB service so you can proceed with confidence. We recommend verification before finalizing design or construction contracts.

How do I get a quote for my project?

Contact us via the contact form, WhatsApp, or email with your location, approximate plot/built-up area, and type of project (residential/commercial/interior). We will schedule a free consultation and then send a formal proposal with scope, deliverables, and pricing.

Do you work outside Dhaka?

Yes. We have completed projects in Sylhet, Chittagong, Comilla, and other districts. For out-of-Dhaka sites we coordinate local approvals and may arrange site visits at key stages. NRB clients often build in their home districts; we manage everything remotely with local coordination.

What is your typical project timeline?

From first meeting to completed drawings, 3–6 months is typical for residential projects (depending on complexity and approval time). Construction duration depends on size and scope; we provide a timeline estimate in the proposal and track it through construction management.

Are you RAJUK certified?

Yes. We are registered and experienced in preparing and submitting RAJUK-compliant drawings and documentation. We handle the full approval process and respond to any queries from the authority on your behalf.

Can I see past projects before hiring?

Yes. Visit our Portfolio page for a selection of residential, commercial, interior, and NRB projects. During the consultation we can also share more examples relevant to your project type and location.

Do you offer sustainable or green design?

Yes. We incorporate passive design, natural ventilation, rainwater harvesting, solar readiness, and locally sourced materials where appropriate. Sustainable options can be discussed in the concept phase and reflected in the design and specifications.

How do I schedule a free consultation?

Use the contact page to request a free 30-minute consultation: fill in your name, email, phone, and project details, and we will confirm a slot (including options for evenings/weekends for NRB clients in different time zones). You can also message us on WhatsApp for a quick response.

What if I need to make changes during design?

We include a defined number of revision rounds in each package. Additional revisions can be agreed for a fee. We recommend finalizing the brief in the discovery phase to minimize changes later, but we remain flexible for reasonable design updates before construction starts.

Who will be my point of contact?

You will have a dedicated project architect or relationship manager. For NRB clients this is your single point of contact for WhatsApp, calls, and reports. For Premium architectural packages we assign a dedicated project architect. You will be introduced at the start of the project.

Can't find your question? Ask us directly or chat on WhatsApp.
