Custom Furniture Designs

Bespoke furniture crafted by Bangladeshi artisans — designed to fit your space perfectly.

Collections

Furniture by Category

Each piece is designed to order — custom dimensions, materials, and finishes to match your interior.

Living Room Furniture

Custom sofas, sectionals, coffee tables, TV consoles, and accent chairs — crafted with premium Bangladeshi teak and imported upholstery fabrics.

Bedroom Furniture

King-size beds, upholstered headboards, bedside tables, dressing units, and walk-in wardrobe systems with soft-close mechanisms.

Dining Room Furniture

Hardwood dining tables from 4 to 12 seats, bench seating, buffet cabinets, and custom bar counters with granite or marble tops.

Office & Study Furniture

Ergonomic desks, executive chairs, built-in bookshelves, filing cabinets, and conference tables for home offices and corporate spaces.

Kitchen & Storage

Modular kitchen cabinets, pantry systems, island counters, shoe cabinets, and built-in wardrobes — all with premium hardware and finishes.

Custom Built-Ins

Floor-to-ceiling bookshelves, window seats, entertainment centres, and under-stair storage — designed to maximise every centimetre of your space.

Process

Our 5-Step Furniture Design Process

1. Consultation & Measurement

On-site visit to measure your space and understand your style preferences, daily routines, and storage needs.

2. Design & 3D Rendering

Detailed 3D models showing your furniture in-situ — choose from materials, colours, and hardware options.

3. Material Selection

Visit our material library to touch and feel actual wood samples, fabrics, leathers, and hardware finishes.

4. Crafting & Quality Control

Built by skilled Bangladeshi artisans with imported machinery — multi-point quality inspection at every stage.

5. Delivery & Installation

White-glove delivery, professional installation, and final adjustments — we don't leave until everything is perfect.
