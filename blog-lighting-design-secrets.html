Lighting Design Secrets: How to Transform Any Room in Your Home

Priya Sen · Nov 28, 2025 · 7 min read

Share:FacebookXLinkedIn

Lighting can make or break a room. The right mix of ambient, task, and accent lighting creates mood, supports daily activities, and can make spaces feel larger and more inviting. Here are professional techniques we use in our interior projects across Bangladeshi homes.

Layer Your Light

Ambient light provides general illumination — ceiling fixtures, cove lights, or natural daylight. Task lighting focuses on work areas: kitchen counters, desks, reading nooks. Accent lighting highlights art, plants, or architecture. Layering all three gives flexibility for different times of day and occasions.

Control and Colour Temperature

Dimmers and multiple circuits let you adjust intensity. Warm white (2700–3000K) suits living and bedrooms; cooler white (4000K+) works in kitchens and bathrooms. We specify fixtures and controls that are available locally and easy to maintain.
