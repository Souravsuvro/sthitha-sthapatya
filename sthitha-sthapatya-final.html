Our Story

Founded on Heritage, Built for the Future

Established in 2002, Sthitha Sthapatya was born from a vision to create architecture that respects Bangladesh's rich cultural heritage while embracing the best of contemporary global design. Our founder, Ar. Rahul Chakraborty, trained at BUET and the Architectural Association in London, bringing a unique cross-cultural perspective to every project.

The name "স্থিত স্থাপত্য" (Sthitha Sthapatya) means "Enduring Architecture" — a philosophy reflected in every structure we design. Over 24 years, we have completed 350+ projects, from private villas in Gulshan to commercial complexes in Chittagong, and served 200+ NRB families seeking to build their dream homes from abroad.

Architectural Consultancy

End-to-end architectural services from site analysis to construction documentation & RAJUK approvals.

Our Process

5-Phase Design Journey

A proven methodology that transforms your vision into detailed, buildable plans.

Phase 1: Discovery & Site Analysis

Comprehensive site survey, soil assessment coordination, RAJUK regulations review, client brief development, and feasibility study including budget estimation.

Phase 2: Concept Design

Multiple design concepts exploring spatial arrangements, massing, orientation for natural ventilation, and initial 3D visualizations for client review and feedback.

Phase 3: Design Development

Refined architectural drawings, structural engineering coordination, MEP planning, material specification, interior layout integration, and detailed 3D renders.

Phase 4: Approvals & Documentation

RAJUK submission drawings, building permit applications, municipal compliance documentation, and all regulatory coordination until approval is secured.

Phase 5: Construction Support

Tender documentation, contractor selection assistance, periodic site visits, construction quality audits, and design clarifications through project completion.

NRB Property & Design Services

Your trusted partner in Bangladesh — build, design, and manage your property from anywhere in the world.

For NRBs

We Bridge the Distance Between You and Your Dream Home

Over 200 Non-Resident Bangladeshi families across the UK, USA, Canada, Middle East, and Australia have trusted Sthitha Sthapatya to manage their property projects in Bangladesh. Our dedicated NRB division handles everything — from land verification and architectural design through construction and final handover — so you never need to worry about distance.

We understand the unique challenges NRBs face: time zone differences, trust concerns, regulatory complexity, and the emotional weight of building a family home from thousands of miles away. That's why we built a service model specifically designed for transparency, accountability, and peace of mind.
